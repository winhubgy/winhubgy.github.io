About WinterHub IT Solutions

Affordable, Reliable, Professional Tech Solutions

At WinterHub IT Solutions, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality technology, no matter their budget. Specializing in Computer Repairs, Laptop Repairs, CCTV Camera Installations, and ordering PC parts, we are committed to providing top-notch tech services that are both affordable and reliable.

Our Story

Founded by Wayne Winter, WinterHub IT Solutions was born out of a passion for helping people make the most of their technology. Wayne has been fixing computers since 2005 and has always been driven by a desire to help others—particularly those who might not have the resources to afford expensive tech solutions. His vision for WinterHub is simple: to ensure that the less fortunate can have access to good systems for as little money as possible, without compromising on quality.

Our Mission

Our mission at WinterHub IT Solutions is to provide professional, trustworthy, and affordable services delivered by tech experts. We are dedicated to making sure our customers have the tech support they need, whether it’s fixing a computer, setting up security cameras, or sourcing the best parts for their systems.

Our Core Values

  • Reliability: We ensure that every solution we provide is dependable and built to last.
  • Efficiency: We work quickly and effectively to minimize downtime for our clients.
  • Customer Service: We prioritize clear communication and personalized support for every client.

Meet Wayne Winter

WinterHub IT Solutions is led by Wayne Winter, a tech enthusiast with nearly two decades of experience. Wayne’s love for technology, combined with his dedication to helping others, is the driving force behind the company’s success. Whether you need a simple repair or a custom tech solution, Wayne is here to help you every step of the way.
